How to Use this Notebook

The purpose of this notebook is to provide you with an online space to jot down key ideas, takeaways, and thoughts about what the presenter covers in each section of the workshop. Because your notes are saved locally in your browser, you can refer back to them during the Certification activity.

⚡ Auto-Saving: Your notes save automatically to your browser cache on every keystroke. Refreshing the page or closing the tab will not lose your text.

📏 No Limits: There are no character constraints on the textboxes. Enter as much information as you need.

↕️ Scroll & Resize: If your notes exceed the textbox height, an internal scrollbar will appear letting you scroll up and down freely. You can also drag the bottom-right corner of any box to physically expand it.

💾 Exporting: Click the "Download My Notes (.txt)" button at the bottom of the page at any time to compile and save your complete notebook to your computer.
